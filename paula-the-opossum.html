Paula the Possum - Level Design

November 7, 2025

Paula the Possum is a retro 3D platformer game currently being developed in Unreal Engine 5, an environment which I feel a lot of gratitude to be contributing to. The creative freedom and attitude among the developers is amazing, and this allowed me to learn the tools to make a level blockout within a month. 

I created the blockout of one of the maps by following a premade concept with a vista, using Landscape and Scythe, which is a level design plugin based on Valve's Hammer Editor. It took me some time to get used to the tools and understand how movement and mechanics worked in order to properly apply them to the blockout.

First stage of the blockout - Vista with a delimited explorable area.

Second stage - Interior spaces and verticality.

Third stage - More complex shapes for the environment.

Fourth stage - Adding water leveling for progression/environmental changes.

Final stage of the blockout - Refining details and enemy encounters.
